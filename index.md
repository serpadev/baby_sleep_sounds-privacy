## Information We Collect

### 1. Audio Recordings (Optional Feature)

Baby Sleep Sounds includes an optional feature that allows you to record custom sounds (such as your voice) to help soothe your baby.

- **What we collect:** Audio recordings created by you using the in-app recording feature
- **How it's stored:** All recordings are stored locally on your device only
- **How it's used:** Recordings are used solely for playback within the app
- **Sharing:** We do NOT upload, transmit, or share your recordings with anyone
- **Deletion:** You can delete recordings at any time through the app

**Important:** The `RECORD_AUDIO` permission is only used when you explicitly choose to record a custom sound. We never record audio without your knowledge or consent.

### 2. App Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data to improve app performance and user experience:

- **Session counts:** Number of times you open the app (used to show upgrade prompts)
- **Preferences:** Theme settings (dark/light mode), favorite sounds
- **How it's stored:** Locally on your device using SharedPreferences
- **Sharing:** This data is never transmitted or shared

### 3. Advertising Data

Baby Sleep Sounds displays ads to free users through Google AdMob.

- **What AdMob collects:** Device identifiers, IP address, app usage data
- **How it's used:** To serve personalized ads and measure ad performance
- **Third party:** Google AdMob is a third-party service with its own privacy policy
- **Control:** You can limit ad personalization through your device settings or upgrade to Premium to remove ads

**AdMob Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 4. Purchase Data (Premium Users)

If you purchase the Premium version:

- **What's collected:** Purchase transaction data (handled by Google Play)
- **Purpose:** To verify your Premium status and unlock features
- **Third party:** Google Play handles all payment processing
- **We do NOT collect:** Credit card numbers or payment information

**Google Play Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Permissions Explained

Baby Sleep Sounds requests the following Android permissions:

| Permission | Purpose | Required? |
|------------|---------|-----------|
| `INTERNET` | Display ads (free users), verify Premium status | Yes |
| `WAKE_LOCK` | Keep screen on during sound playback | Yes |
| `RECORD_AUDIO` | Record custom sounds (optional feature) | No - only when you use recording |
| `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` | Save custom recordings | No - only when you use recording |
| `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` | Access saved recordings | No - only when you use recording |
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` | Continue playing sounds in background | Yes |

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only

All your personal data is stored locally on your device:
- Audio recordings
- Favorite sounds
- App preferences
- Session counts

This data is NOT:
- ❌ Uploaded to our servers (we don't have servers)
- ❌ Shared with third parties (except AdMob for ads)
- ❌ Backed up to cloud services

When you uninstall Baby Sleep Sounds:
